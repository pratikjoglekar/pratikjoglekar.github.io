MassMutual App —

iOS and Android App

As a product designer, I lead the design effort, including stakeholder management, planning, user research, wireframe design, visual design, usability testing, and VQA to create the entire product from inception. The premise of this app is to make it consumer-facing yet being true to the business requirements and expectations. Significant features include dividend withdrawal, auto payments, requesting loans, transaction status, and managing beneficiaries currently in development. The app is also under a redesign process - that would entail satisfying customer expectations being collected from user interviews and analytical information.

Authenticated Dashboard & Product Pages —

Web

As a Designer, the team felt a need to redesign the dashboard, policy, and product pages. The hypothesis was to make the landing page of the authenticated experience far more user-friendly with more available interactions and redesign the legacy experience of the following product/policy pages. I participated in the end-to-end dashboard redesign and led the product/policy redesign process of stakeholder and customer interviews, wireframe and user study, and visual design..

IVR Experience —

Voice

Creating a conversational IVR experience that would support MassMutual products and policies, enabling customers to complete transactions in the IVR. I started with competitive analysis, convinced the team to move from traditional to conversational IVR, and later discussed adding Annuity Product support with stakeholders and conducting interviews to identify the need.

Paperless Experience —

Web

Massmutual's vision for paperless is to make all the documents available online. I collaborated with the product team to create strategies for acquisition, end-to-end experience - identifying existing experience drawbacks, wireframing, and user study.
